Introduction of Kings Club Sports Facilities

Among Bangalore’s top sports clubs, King’s Club is among the most elite and luxurious. Here you can enjoy the lavish facilities made especially for keeping you on your toes while learning and enjoying various sports. 

 

A fitness academy has been designed to make you feel like a king. Our facility also includes a fully equipped Gym, Yoga center, a spacious exercise room, and a dance academy.  Our trained coaches will keep you engaged, motivated, and passionate by guiding and motivating you. The game court is an auditorium where you can show off your skills.  Whatever your sport is, whether it’s swimming, table tennis, or badminton, you’ll be able to enjoy it here.

Get training with the industry’s best instructors, gain access to facilities all day for a quick after-work match, meet fellow members, and even build business relationships. 

 

King’s Club’s sports academy energizes and strengthens your practice, setting your practice at the pace you need it.  Go one-on-one with personal coaching or take group fitness classes today. A superbly designed sports pace will get you started, in shape,  or energized.

Join today for group fitness classes, go one-on-one with personal coaching. Get started, get in shape, or get energized at a superbly designed sports pace.
