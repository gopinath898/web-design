FAQ

1. How can I get a Kings Club Membership in Bangalore?
All you need to do is just give us a call or drop us a message and we’ll get back to you as soon as possible.
2. What is the membership fee for Kings Club?

Rs. 5 Lakhs for family of 4 People For 10 Years. (Excl. of GST)

3. Is the membership transferable?
NO
4. If the membership is in my name then how many members from my family are allowed to use the facility?
