Introduction of Banquet

The Kings Club Banquet features beautiful interiors and a delicious menu. Stunning outdoor garden with enough space to accommodate large gatherings offering an array of delicacies for events, our catering services provide delicious meals for any event can be tailored to suit its requirements.

Whether hosting a seminar, an exhibition, or a meeting, you will find your perfect venue here weddings and receptions.

Our elegant and high-class banquets offer extensive and flexible spaces to organize a variety of  functions and parties.  We have established our positive reputation based on our outstanding service, exemplary staff, and exclusive offers, which allows you to fully enjoy your events with no worries.

Indoor Party Hall

Our indoor party hall is ideal for birthday parties & other social get-togethers. This pace allows you to organize your events in a unique way and let your guests mesmerized with the decorations & food.

Outdoor Wedding Hall

Our outdoor wedding hall is very beautiful & fascinating with thousands of gorgeous flowers and lots of lush greenery. Extra decoration can also be arranged to customize the outdoor wedding hall to meet your exact requirements.
