Functional Academics Group

Pre Vocational Skills and Communication

In addition to our core program, we offer a specialized Functional Academics Group. This group focuses on:
1. Pre Vocational Skills: Equipping students with practical skills that prepare them for future vocational endeavors.
2. Communication Skills: Cultivating effective communication is at the core of our approach, fostering meaningful interactions.

Life Skills

Our emphasis on life skills extends beyond academics. We guide students in developing essential life skills, ensuring their readiness for various aspects of daily living.
At Perseverance School, we are committed to creating an inclusive and supportive learning environment where each child's potential is recognized and nurtured. Join us on this transformative journey!
