Founder

Aashish

Aashish Anand, a BITS Pilani alum, is a software engineer and social entrepreneur committed to fostering positive change. As the founder of Perseverance Special School, Aashish integrates social responsibility into education, creating an inclusive environment beyond traditional norms. With extensive experience working with children with special needs, Aashish's leadership at Perseverance Special School goes beyond academics, emphasizing support and empowerment for every student. He stays current with the latest insights by completing a specialized certificate course focused on learning difficulties. Aashish actively engages with the community, organizing workshops, seminars, and outreach programs. This collective effort raises awareness and advocates for the rights of individuals with special needs, contributing to a more inclusive society.

Our Chairman

Dr. Suchismita Das(Anand)

Dr. Suchismita Das (Anand), a trailblazer in the field of Special Education, boasts an illustrious career spanning over 18 years, marked by a remarkable commitment to transformative education. As the esteemed founder of Perseverance Special School and the dedicated Managing Trustee of Pragnya Charitable Trust, Dr. Das stands as a beacon of dedication, excellence, and global impact.
Educational Foundation:
Dr. Das's journey commenced with an exceptional academic foundation, culminating in the attainment of a Doctorate of Philosophy in Special Education from Central Christian University, Malawi. This academic achievement not only underscores her intellectual prowess but also highlights her profound empathy for the distinctive needs of individuals with disabilities.

Our Chairman

Srivaishnavi

Srivaishnavi, our Head of School and a devoted trustee member at Perseverance, blends a diverse educational background, including Engineering, a Master's in Psychology, and a PGD in Special Education. Her journey reflects a profound passion for understanding and supporting children. Transitioning from the corporate world, Srivaishnavi is driven by a deep desire to actively contribute to children's development. Focused on fostering holistic growth, she envisions each child evolving into an independent, content adult, enjoying equal opportunities. Within Perseverance, Srivaishnavi leads with an unwavering commitment. Her mission is clear — ensuring every child, regardless of challenges, receives the care and resources needed to thrive. At the core is the creation of a joyful environment where each child's unique potential is recognized and nurtured.

Technical Director

Sanchita Das

Sanchita Das is a seasoned and devoted educator, bringing a wealth of experience to her teaching career, which spans several years. She has been actively involved in instructing students in English (SL) from grades VII to XII. Her commitment goes beyond the classroom, as she has played a significant role in school administration, serving as a member of the Managing Committee for 12 years and contributing as the secretary of the Academic Council. Sanchita has also demonstrated her supervisory skills by successfully overseeing the Madhyamik (10th) Board Examination as a Venue Supervisor, taking charge as the Head Examiner for the Higher Secondary (+2) examination in West Bengal and condacting teachers' training as the Key Resource Person organisddy by the Govt of W. B. During her developmental years as a teacher, she taught English as a first language, while handling the responsibilities of a a House Mother, organizing annual programs and guiding students in various co-curricular activities.
