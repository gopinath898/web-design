Welcome To Our School

Kindergarten & Pre-Cadet School

Our commitment is centered on ensuring that neurodiverse children receive equal opportunities in social interactions, academic proficiency, and participation in events and activities, equipping them to navigate the world with confidence. At Perseverance, we recognize the unique needs of each child, providing one-on-one interventions for those requiring additional support in developing both physical and mental skills necessary for collaborative group work.
In adherence to our dedication to quality education, we maintain a steadfast classroom ratio. This deliberate choice enables our educators to offer individualized attention to each child, fostering holistic development across skills, specialized assistance such as sensory or behavioral modifications, and academic pursuits. Vision

Our vision is to reinvigorate the purpose of education for children with special needs by inspiring positivity, promoting learning, cultivating empathy, and fostering inclusion in the community. We believe that every individual has the potential to make a positive impact on the lives of children with special needs and to contribute to their growth and development.
