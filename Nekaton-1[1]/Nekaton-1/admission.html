Tuition Fees

At Perseverance School, we are committed to providing quality education that is accessible to all. Our tuition fees are competitive and vary based on the program and level of education. For detailed information on the fees, please contact our admissions team.

20% Reserved Seats for Economically Backward Children

In our dedication to inclusivity, Perseverance School reserves 20% of seats for economically backward children. These reserved seats come with a scholarship that provides a concession on tuition fees. We firmly believe in creating opportunities for every child to access transformative education, regardless of financial constraints.

1

Contact Admissions:

Reach out to our admissions team for detailed information on tuition fees for your child's specific program and level.

2

Discuss Scholarship Opportunities:

Inquire about the reserved seats for economically backward children and the corresponding scholarship during your admission consultation.

3

Customized Payment Plans:

We understand that financial situations may vary. Our team is available to discuss customized payment plans to ensure that your child's education is both enriching and affordable.

4

Maintaining Privacy:

We respect the confidentiality of financial matters. Rest assured, your inquiries about fees and scholarship opportunities will be handled discreetly and professionally.
