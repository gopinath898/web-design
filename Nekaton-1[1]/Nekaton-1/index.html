Welcome to Perseverance

Working Towards Inclusion

where we firmly believe that every child, whether neurotypical or neurodiverse, is entitled to a fundamental education. Our mission at Perseverance is to extend comprehensive support to neurodiverse children, offering them not just education but also tailored assistance in developing pre-skills, addressing sensory needs through Occupational Therapy, enhancing communication abilities, and implementing behavior modification strategies seamlessly integrated into our school program module.

Join us at Perseverance, where we are committed to nurturing the potential of every child and preparing them for a future of pride andÂ achievement.
