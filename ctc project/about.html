About Us

At Calcutta Tube Centre, we recognize the importance of sustainable practices and our responsibility towards the environment. As part of our ongoing commitment to eco-friendly solutions, we are proud to announce that our new warehouse in Nelmangala is partially powered by solar energy. Embracing solar technology not only aligns with our values of innovation and sustainability but also represents a significant step towards reducing our carbon footprint. By harnessing the clean, renewable energy of the sun, we are able to minimize our reliance on conventional power sources, thereby contributing to a greener future. This initiative is more than just an environmental endeavor; it reflects our broader vision to integrate sustainability into every aspect of our business. From the products we offer to the practices we follow, our focus on environmental stewardship is unwavering. The incorporation of solar energy at our warehouse is just one example of how we are actively working towards a more sustainable tomorrow. We continually explore opportunities to implement eco-friendly practices across our operations, demonstrating our family-owned business's commitment to social responsibility and ethical growth. Our solar-powered warehouse symbolizes our belief that business success and environmental protection can go hand in hand. It underscores our dedication to delivering quality products and services while being mindful of the world we live in. Join us in our journey towards a cleaner, greener, and more sustainable future.

About Us

Calcutta Tube Centre was established in 1974 by its founder Mr Sawarmal Agarwal as a Registered Distributor for M.S,G.I,G.P,C.R Pipes C.I, MS, GM Valves Fittings and PVC, CPVC Pipes & Fittings. The office is headquartered in Bangalore, India. Over the last four decades, the company has created a niche for themselves by understanding and catering to the needs of leading Builders, Construction companies, Engineers, and Corporates. The company also has a wide network of dealers across Karnataka. Calcutta Tube Centre specializes in the complete range of high-quality Plumbing Materials, Engineering works, Agricultural Valves Fittings, Pipes & Rainwater Harvesting systems and are the preferred choice of thousands of customers for the variety and service provided. The aim has always been to understand the needs of the customers and deliver the most optimised solutions both in terms of quality and price. Calcutta Tube Centre is the authorised distributors for APL APOLLO, Surya Roshani, MSL, JSW COLOURN+,JSW PRAGTHI+,JSW VISHWAS+, JSW KALINGA,JINDALHISSAR,JINDALSTAR,TRUFLO By hindware ZOLOTO,AVR, VS and NVR Brand Products which means that the entire plumbing range practically - GI and MS pipes and fittings, PVC, uPVC, CPVC, Bendable Pipes and fittings and all kinds of domestic and industrial valves- are all available under one roof! The company also deals with the entire range of hardware and wet support system required for Agriculture. Over the next few years, the company aims at diversifying by adding FRP chambers and covers and adhesives to our inventory.
