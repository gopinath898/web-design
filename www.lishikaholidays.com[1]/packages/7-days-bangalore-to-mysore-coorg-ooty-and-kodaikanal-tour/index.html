7 Days Bangalore to Mysore, Coorg, Ooty and Kodaikanal Tour

  • Mysore, Coorg, Ooty and Kodaikanal
Durations

7 Days

Price: for more price details contact Nandi Tour and Travels

Embark on an unforgettable seven-day odyssey from Bangalore to Mysore, Ooty, Coorg, and Kodaikanal with Lishika Holidays. Experience the regal splendour of Mysore, the serene beauty of Ooty’s hill stations, the lush greenery of Coorg’s coffee plantations, and the tranquil charm of Kodaikanal. With comfortable accommodations and expert guidance throughout, this journey promises an immersive exploration of South India’s diverse landscapes and cultural heritage with Lishika Holidays.

Day 01:

ARRIVE BANGALORE TO MYSORE
Pickup from arrival and proceed to Mysore. En route visit Srirangapatna(Dariya Daulat Bagh, Gumbaz, Tippu Fort and Sri Ranganathaswamy Temple). On arrival check in to hotel. Evening proceed to Brindavan Garden (botanical park with musical fountain). Overnight stay at Mysore.
Distance between Bangalore to Mysore is 160 Kms (3.5 hours) by road

Day 02:

MYSORE TO COORG
After breakfast, checkout and proceed to Coorg enroute visit Dubare Elephant Camp(Elephant safari extra payable directly), Namdroling Monastery Golden Temple (Tibetian Golden Temple), NisargaDhama. On arrival check in to a hotel. Evening visit Raja’s Seat. Overnight stay at Coorg.
Distance between Mysore to Coorg is 135 Kms (3.5 hours) by road

Day 03:

COORG LOCAL SIGHTSEEING
After breakfast proceed to Tala Kaveri Temple, Bhagamandala (Bhagandeshwara Temple), Omkareshwara Temple, Madikeri Fort and Abbey Falls. Return back to hotel. Overnight stay at Coorg.

Day 04:

COORG TO OOTY LOCAL SIGHTSEEING
After breakfast checkout and proceed to Ooty, on arrival check in to hotel. Visit Rose Garden, Ooty Lake and Botanical Garden. Overnight stay at Ooty.
Distance between Coorg to Ooty is 265 Kms / 6.5 Hours by road

Day 05:

OOTY LOCAL
After breakfast Proceed to Coonoor. Coonoor Sight seeing(Sim’s Park, lam’s Rock, Dolphin Nose, then travel to kodaikenal stay over night

Day 06:

Kodaikanal Local
After morning breakfast visit to Cookers walk, Green valley view, and the Kodai Lake – Star Shaped Lake and Bryant Park. Overnight stay at Kodaikanal.

Day 07:

Kodaikanal Local Sightseeing

After morning breakfast, proceed to sightseeing to visit Pillar’s Rocks Silent Valley View, Berijam lake view, Silver cascade falls, Devil’s Kitchen – popularly known as the Guna Caves, Pine Forest, Kurinji Andavar Temple. Overnight stay at Kodaikanal.
Note:-Berijam Lake View, we need to take permission prior to the entry. We will try to cover otherwise we will skip this sightseeing point. Then drop to Bangalore
