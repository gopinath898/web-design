MICE

Area 83 has become a sought-after destination for MICE events, offering a range of state-of-the-art facilities and amenities to ensure that your event is a success. Whether you're planning a business meeting, conference, trade show, or incentive trip, our team of professionals will work with you to create a bespoke experience that meets your needs.

Our event spaces are designed to cater to all types of MICE events, ranging from intimate meetings to large-scale conferences. Our venues are equipped with the latest audiovisual systems, lighting, and sound equipment, making them perfect for hosting presentations, workshops, and seminars.

Area83 spacious life for corporate event

Area 83 offers a spacious life for corporate and MICE events, providing a luxurious and comfortable environment that promotes productivity and relaxation. Our event spaces are designed to provide ample room for attendees to move around and interact comfortably. This spaciousness is particularly crucial for events that require attendees to be seated for long periods.

Our event spaces at Area 83 are designed to cater to all types of corporate and MICE events. The venues are equipped with state-of-the-art technology and amenities, such as audiovisual systems, lighting, and sound equipment, to ensure that your event runs smoothly. The spacious layout of our venues also provides ample opportunities for branding and customization, creating a personalized environment for your event.

The spaciousness of Area 83 extends beyond the event spaces to our accommodation and common areas. Our luxurious guest rooms and suites are designed to provide maximum comfort, with ample space for relaxation and rejuvenation. Additionally, our common areas such as the lobby, lounge, and restaurants are designed to provide a spacious and welcoming atmosphere, perfect for attendees to network and socialize.
