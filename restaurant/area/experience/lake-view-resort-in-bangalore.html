Lake View Resort in Bangalore

Are you ready to escape the hustle and bustle of the city and immerse yourself in the serene beauty of nature? Look no further, for you have arrived at Area83, the most idyllic lake view resort in Bangalore.

90% greenery surrounds our resort. It also features a large 18-acre private lake. This creates a peaceful and beautiful setting for your perfect getaway.

Experience Unforgettable Times at Lake Views

At Area83, we understand that every moment of your vacation is precious. With breathtaking lake views right from your accommodation, we guarantee that you'll cherish your times at lake views forever.

Imagine waking up to the soft sunlight reflecting on the lake. Or, picture yourself relaxing as you watch a stunning sunset over the water. These are memories that will stay with you forever.

Check-In and Check-Out Times Made Convenient

We value your time, and we want to make your stay as hassle-free as possible. Our flexible check-in and check-out times ensure that you can plan your trip at your convenience. Our flexible schedule is great for people who prefer to wake up early or stay up late. It allows you to make the most of your day or have a relaxed morning.

Discover Our Resort in Bangalore

Area83 is more than just a resort; it's an experience that caters to all your senses. We have many types of rooms to choose from, making us one of the top resorts in Bangalore.

Chalet Stays: Immerse yourself in the charm of our cozy chalets, nestled amidst the lush greenery. These rustic yet comfortable abodes will make you feel at home while still enjoying the luxuries of a top-notch resort.

Suite Rooms: Indulge in luxury with our elegant suite rooms, thoughtfully designed to provide you with a lavish experience. Enjoy modern amenities and stunning views, ensuring your stay is nothing short of remarkable.

Private Villa: For the ultimate privacy and luxury, our private villas are the perfect choice. These private villas in a natural setting provide a peaceful escape to relax and make lasting memories with family and friends.

Embark on Thrilling Water Sports Activities

At Area83, we take your adventure quotient seriously. Our 11-acre private lake is not just for picturesque views; it's also the playground for thrilling water sports activities. Whether you're a water sports enthusiast or a first-time adventurer, our range of water activities will leave you excited.

Kayaking: Glide through the pristine waters and feel the cool breeze on your face as you kayak along the lake's edge. It's a fantastic way to explore the beauty of the lake up close.

Boating: Set sail on a boating adventure and soak in the tranquility of the surroundings. Our boats are safe, comfortable, and offer a unique perspective of the resort.

Water Cycle: For a fun-filled experience with family and friends, try our water cycles. These pedal-powered contraptions are perfect for some light-hearted fun on the water.

A Green Paradise

Area83 is proud to be a haven for nature lovers. With 90% greenery, our resort is a green paradise that fosters a sense of connection with the natural world. Stroll through our lush gardens, breathe in the fresh air, and let the beauty of nature rejuvenate your soul.

Unwind and Recharge

Whether you seek adventure or tranquility, Area83 has it all. Discover the joy of a lakeside retreat in Bangalore that offers a perfect blend of relaxation and recreation. Soak in the beauty of the surrounding landscape, indulge in thrilling activities, and create lasting memories with your loved ones.

Book Your Retreat Today

Escape the city's chaos and find solace in the lap of nature at Area83. Book your stay today and treat yourself to an experience like no other. Our team is dedicated to ensuring your comfort and satisfaction throughout your stay, making your visit unforgettable.

Come, experience the magic of lake view resort in Bangalore at Area83 - Your Riverside Resort in Bangalore!

Here are some of the key features:

  • Spectacular Lake Views: Indulge in mesmerizing lake views from the comfort of your accommodation, creating unforgettable memories of tranquil sunrises and breathtaking sunsets.
  • Lush Green Surroundings: Experience the bliss of being surrounded by 90% greenery, providing a serene and rejuvenating environment that connects you with nature.
  • Perfect for Events: Host memorable events, weddings, or corporate gatherings with our picturesque lakeside venues and impeccable event planning services.
  • Wellness Retreat: Recharge your mind and body at our wellness center, offering rejuvenating spa treatments and yoga sessions amidst the serene ambiance.
  • Exquisite Dining: Relish delectable cuisine at our on-site restaurant, offering a delightful blend of local and international flavors.
