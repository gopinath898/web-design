Cancellation Policy

If the event is canceled more than 1 week (7 days) in advance then no cancellation charges will be applicable

If the event is canceled 5 to 7 days in advance then you will be liable to pay 25% of the total package cost for the minimum guaranteed pax

If the event is canceled 3 to 5 days in advance then you will be liable to pay 50% of the total package cost for the minimum guaranteed pax

If the event is canceled 2 to 3 days in advance then you will be liable to pay 75% of the total package cost for the minimum guaranteed pax

If the event is canceled less than 2 days in advance then you will be liable to pay 100% of the total package cost for the minimum guaranteed pax
