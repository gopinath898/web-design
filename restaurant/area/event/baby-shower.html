Overview

Motherhood, what a blessing! You're counting the days until your little one makes a grand entrance into the world. The days and months before the birth of a baby are truly an exciting time period.

If you are parents-to-be you would undoubtedly want to celebrate the arrival of your little one with your closest family members and friends. A baby shower is a great way to gather and express your well wishes to the growing family. It is a perfect ceremony to "shower" the mom-to-be with love and good wishes. Trust us, she needs it! Every mother needs to be celebrated.

There are no hard-and-fast rules when it comes to a baby shower other than the fact that they are usually held four to six weeks before the baby’s due date. The main dilemma is choosing the spot for the baby shower. If you are in or around Bangalore and are feeling outdoorsy, we can help you. (P.S. We can help you if you feel indoorsy too, but outdoors is more fun, right?)

 

When it comes to finding a spot to host your outdoor baby shower, you need a place where you can comfortably seat everyone on your guest list without them feeling cramped. You should consider bringing the party to Area 83; we could give you a sweet deal on a spot overlooking our lake. You can imagine the whole setting. It is a sight for sore eyes. Anyone who is a part of your baby shower will never forget the experience. And we are certain that the MVP (mom-to-be) will definitely enjoy a break away from the commotion of the city.

 

If you are planning a baby shower, do not hesitate to let us know your plans so we can ensure that everything is set according to your standards. Decoration? Check. Theme? Check. Fancy lights? Check. Dinner by the lake? Check. We are at your service.
