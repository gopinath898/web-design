Bengali Wedding at Area83: Embracing Tradition and Culture

Events | Sep 27 2023 | By Rafsal Ahammed
  • share:

At Area83, we celebrate the tapestry of love, and a Bengali wedding is a vivid and intricate thread in this beautiful fabric. Rich in tradition and vibrant in celebration, a Bengali wedding, also known as "Biye," is an awe-inspiring experience. Let's embark on a journey through the colorful customs and rituals that make a Bengali wedding a cherished affair.

Aiburo Bhat: The Celebration of Singledom

In the lead-up to the grand Bengali wedding, the bride and the groom are celebrated separately. Aiburo Bhat, which translates to last meal as a bachelor/bachelorette is a delightful occasion. The bride and groom are treated to sumptuous meals by their respective families. At Area83, we provide a perfect setting for these celebrations, where the joy of impending union fills the air.

Gaye Holud: The Turmeric Ceremony

Gaye Holud, the turmeric ceremony, is a vibrant and joyous event in Bengali weddings. The bride and groom are adorned with a paste of turmeric, sandalwood, and mustard oil, signifying purification and blessings. At Area83, our open and picturesque spaces make for an ideal location for this colorful and cheerful ceremony.

Mala Badal: The Exchange of Garlands

The Mala Badal ceremony is where the bride and groom exchange garlands, symbolizing their acceptance of each other as life partners. It's a moment filled with anticipation and joy, witnessed by family and friends. Area83, as a preferred Bengali wedding venue in Bangalore, offers a charming backdrop for this heartwarming tradition.

Saat Paak: The Sacred Circumambulation

The Saat Paak is a sacred fire ceremony where the bride, adorned in a traditional red and white saree, circles the groom seven times. Each circle represents a vow and a promise for a blissful and harmonious life together. The warmth and tranquility of our resort provide a serene atmosphere for this sacred ritual.

Traditional Feast: Savoring Bengali Delicacies

A Bengali wedding is incomplete without a traditional feast featuring mouthwatering Bengali delicacies. From the aromatic rice and flavorful fish curries to the sweet delights of sandesh and rasgulla, we ensure that your guests savor the authentic flavors of Bengal at Area83.

Perfect Venue for Your Bengali Wedding

Choosing the right venue is crucial for a Bengali wedding, and Area83, as a renowned Bengali wedding venue in Bangalore, offers a picturesque and versatile setting. Whether you envision a traditional outdoor ceremony or an indoor celebration, our spaces can accommodate your needs.

Expert Wedding Planning Team: Your Dream, Our Expertise

Planning a Bengali wedding involves intricate details and precise coordination. At Area83, we have a dedicated team of wedding planners who specialize in Bengali weddings. They take care of everything, allowing you to focus on the cultural richness and joy of your special day.

Capture the Memories: Moments to Cherish Forever

A Bengali wedding is a treasure trove of beautiful moments. Our professional photographers and videographers specialize in capturing the essence of your wedding day. Let them preserve the traditions, the laughter, and the love so that you can relive these moments for years to come.

Your Bengali Wedding Awaits at Area83

At Area83, we don't just host weddings; we create memories. Your Bengali wedding is a journey of tradition, love, and culture, and we are honored to be part of it. Witness the cultural richness of a Bengali wedding at Area83, where every tradition is celebrated with joy and authenticity. Your dream Bengali wedding awaits!
