Resort with 17+ Games in Bangalore

Events | Jun 26 2023 | By Rafsal Ahammed
  • share:

Introduction: 

Welcome to Area83, the premier adventure sport resort in Bangalore! If you're searching for an exhilarating getaway filled with exciting games and activities, look no further. Our resort offers a wide range of thrilling experiences that will leave you with unforgettable memories. Get ready to embark on an adrenaline-pumping journey as we introduce you to the best resort with games in Bangalore!

Unleash Your Adventurous Spirit with Area83’s Games & Fun Activities:

At Area83, we believe in combining thrill with fun and offering a diverse range of activities that cater to every adventure enthusiast. Whether you're an adrenaline junkie or simply looking for a memorable experience, our resort has something for everyone. Let's dive into the exhilarating games and fun activities that await you at Area83:

1. Archery Tag: Aim, Shoot, and Tag! 

Experience the excitement of archery combined with the intensity of a fast-paced tag game. Archery Tag at Area83 pits teams against each other, aiming to hit the opponents' targets while protecting their own. Use your bow and foam-tipped arrows to tag opponents and take them down, all while defending your territory. It's a combat sport that requires agility, accuracy, and teamwork.

2. Bubble Soccer: Bounce, Roll, and Score! 

Get ready for a hilarious twist on traditional soccer. Bubble Soccer at Area83 allows you to step inside giant inflatable bubbles and engage in a bouncing, rolling, and crashing game of soccer. It's a laugh-out-loud experience that guarantees fun-filled moments with your friends and family.

3. Dirt Kart: Rev Up Your Engines! 

Prepare for an adrenaline-fueled ride on our dirt karts. These off-road vehicles are designed to tackle any terrain, offering a thrilling adventure as you navigate through challenging tracks. Get ready to experience speed, control, and the thrill of off-road racing at Area83.

4. Giant Swing: Feel the Rush! 

Suspended high above the ground, our Giant Swing will give you an incredible adrenaline rush and breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape. Brace yourself as you swing through the air, feeling the rush of the wind and the sheer excitement that comes with it. It's an exhilarating experience that will leave you craving more.

5. Human Foosball: Be a Part of the Game! 

Step into a life-sized foosball arena and become a part of the game. Human Foosball at Area83 brings the classic table game to life, allowing you to team up with friends and compete in a life-sized version of this popular sport. Work together to outscore your opponents and enjoy the thrill of being inside a giant foosball table.

6. Kayaking: Paddle Through Serenity! 

Immerse yourself in nature's beauty with kayaking at Area83. Explore the serene surroundings as you paddle through calm waters and navigate gentle currents. It's a refreshing and exciting activity suitable for both beginners and experienced kayakers.

7. Keypunch: Test Your Speed and Agility! 

Challenge your reflexes with Keypunch, a fast-paced game that tests your reaction time and accuracy. Punch the lit-up buttons as quickly as possible and compete against friends or family to achieve the highest score. Keypunch at Area83 guarantees high-energy fun for everyone.

8. Low Rope Course: Conquer the Obstacles! 

Navigate your way through a series of challenging obstacles on our low rope course. Test your balance, coordination, and problem-solving skills as you tackle rope bridges, balancing beams, and tightropes. The low rope course at Area83 encourages teamwork, boosts confidence, and provides an exciting adventure for all.

9. Magic Turtle: Glide Through Twists and Turns! 

Experience the thrill of Magic Turtle at Area83, a unique adventure. This activity is a combination of physical challenges, puzzles, and teamwork that requires participants to think creatively and work together to overcome obstacles.

10. Meltdown: Dodge and Conquer! 

Put your reflexes to the test with Meltdown, a game that challenges your ability to dodge moving obstacles. Jump, duck, and twist your body to avoid swinging bars and make it to the other side. It's a high-energy activity at Area83 that will get your heart racing and adrenaline pumping.

11. Paintball (Min. Age: 18): Engage in Tactical Battles! 

Gear up and engage in an action-packed game of paintball at Area83. Strategize with your team, plan your moves, and unleash your competitive spirit as you navigate through themed battlefields. Experience the thrill of hunting down opponents while utilizing strategy and teamwork to emerge victorious.

12. Pipeline: Slide into Excitement! 

One unique and exciting team building activity that can be implemented in a corporate setting is the Pipeline Team Building Activity, in which the team has to transport marbles from the start line to the finish line using only PVC pipes.

13. Pyramid Builder: Climb, Balance, and Ascend! 

Pyramid Building is an engaging and collaborative activity that puts teamwork and creativity to the test. Participants are challenged to design and construct a large pyramid structure using the resources provided. The objective is to work together to build small prism-like structures that can be skillfully assembled to form a magnificent and sturdy pyramid. It's a captivating exercise that encourages communication, problem-solving, and careful consideration of the overall design.

14. Rocket Ejector: Blast Off into the Sky! 

Prepare for an out-of-this-world experience on our Rocket Ejector. Strap in and get ready to be launched into the sky, feeling the thrill of weightlessness and a surge of adrenaline. The Rocket Ejector at Area83 guarantees an exhilarating adventure that will leave you breathless.

15. Sky Swing: Defy Gravity! 

Soar through the air on our Sky Swing, defying gravity and experiencing the thrill of flight. Feel the wind rush past you as you swing high above the ground, enjoying breathtaking views and an unmatched adrenaline rush. The Sky Swing at Area83 is an unforgettable experience for thrill-seekers.

16. Superman: Fly Like a Superhero! 

Channel your inner superhero with the Superman ride at Area83. Strap into the harness, lie facedown, and experience the sensation of flying through the air. It's a unique adventure that allows you to feel the freedom and exhilaration of flying, just like your favorite superhero.

17. Tree Climbing: Reach New Heights! 

Challenge yourself to reach new heights with tree climbing at Area83. Scale the trees, using ropes and harnesses, and enjoy panoramic views from above. Tree climbing is a thrilling activity that combines physical strength, agility, and a sense of accomplishment.

18. Water Cycle: Glide on Water! 

Enjoy a fun and relaxing ride on our water cycles. Glide across the water, taking in the serene surroundings and enjoying the tranquility of the moment. It's a perfect activity to unwind and connect with nature while having a great time.

Conclusion: 

At Area83, we are dedicated to providing you with the best resort experience in Bangalore, filled with thrilling games and exciting activities. From Archery Tag to Kayaking, our resort offers a wide range of adventures that cater to every thrill-seeker. So, gather your friends and family, visit Area83, and immerse yourself in the excitement and fun of our games and activities. Get ready for an unforgettable adventure that will leave you with cherished memories. Don't miss out on the opportunity to experience the best resort with games and fun activities in Bangalore! Book our adventure packages today!!
