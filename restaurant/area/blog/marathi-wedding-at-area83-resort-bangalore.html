Marathi Wedding at Area83: Celebrate Tradition and Elegance

Events | Sep 28 2023 | By Rafsal Ahammed
  • share:

At Area83, we believe in celebrating the diversity of India's rich cultural heritage. A Marathi wedding is a beautiful blend of simplicity, elegance, and profound traditions. If you're planning a Marathi wedding, let us be your host and guide as we immerse ourselves in the richness of this culture. Experience every ritual, every moment, and every tradition that makes a Marathi wedding truly special.

Antarpat: Unveiling the Bride and Groom

The Antarpat ceremony is a unique and heartwarming tradition in Marathi weddings. It involves the use of a silk shawl or curtain, known as the Antarpat, which separates the bride and groom. As the Antarpat is removed, the couple sees each other for the first time on their wedding day. At Area83, we provide a serene and picturesque setting for this magical moment. Our lakeside views and lush green surroundings create the perfect ambiance to unveil the bride and groom, making it a memory to cherish.

Mangalsutra Dharan: The Sacred Thread Ceremony

The Mangalsutra is not just a piece of jewelry; it's a symbol of love and commitment. In the Mangalsutra Dharan ceremony, the groom ties the sacred thread around the bride's neck, signifying their union. At Area83, we understand the significance of this moment. Our experienced team ensures that every detail, from the choice of the Mangalsutra to the sacred chants, is executed flawlessly.

Vara-Vadhu Pratigya: Exchanging Garlands

The Vara-Vadhu Pratigya is a joyful moment in a Marathi wedding. It involves the exchange of garlands between the bride and groom, symbolizing their acceptance of each other. Area83 provides a beautiful backdrop for this ritual. Our traditional decor and floral arrangements enhance the beauty of this exchange, creating a perfect setting for your Marathi wedding.

Traditional Marathi Feast: Savoring Delicacies

No Marathi wedding is complete without a traditional feast. At Area83, we offer a delectable range of Marathi cuisine to delight your guests. From puran poli to bhakri, vada pav to pav bhaji, our culinary experts curate a menu that showcases the flavors of Maharashtra. Let your guests savor the richness of Marathi culture through their taste buds.

Versatile Venues: Your Dream Wedding Setting

Choosing the right venue for your Marathi wedding is crucial, and Area83 understands the importance of this decision. Our resort, nestled in the heart of nature, provides a serene and picturesque setting for your celebrations. Whether you prefer a traditional outdoor ceremony or an indoor affair, our versatile spaces can accommodate your every need.

Experienced Team: Your Wedding, Our Expertise

Planning a Marathi wedding involves meticulous attention to detail, and at Area83, we have a team of experienced wedding planners who are dedicated to making your dream wedding a reality. From organizing rituals to coordinating logistics, we take care of everything, allowing you to focus on what truly matters – your love and commitment to each other.

Capture the Moments: Memories to Cherish

A Marathi wedding is a tapestry of beautiful moments, and we believe that every moment should be captured. Area83 provides access to professional photographers and videographers who specialize in wedding photography. Let them capture the essence of your love story, ensuring that your memories are preserved for generations to come.

Your Dream Marathi Wedding Awaits

Area83 is not just a wedding venue; it's a place where traditions come to life and love is celebrated with elegance. With the grace of tradition, the warmth of culture, and the beauty of nature, we offer a setting that is perfect for celebrating love the Marathi way. Let us be a part of your journey as you embark on this new chapter of life together. Your dream Marathi wedding awaits at Area83, the premier wedding resort in Bangalore, where tradition and elegance meet in perfect harmony.
