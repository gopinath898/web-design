Best Resort in Bangalore for Corporate Outings

Events | Jun 26 2023 | By Rafsal Ahammed
  • share:

Introduction: 

When it comes to corporate outings, Bangalore offers a plethora of options that combine breathtaking landscapes, luxurious accommodations, and exciting activities. These resorts provide the perfect setting for team building, relaxation, and fostering a sense of camaraderie among employees. In this blog, we will explore in detail the best resort in Bangalore for corporate outings and why we are ideal for hosting such events. Additionally, we will delve into the unique features of Area83, a renowned resort that stands out as an excellent choice for corporate outings, celebrations and other MICE (Meetings, Incentives, Conferences, and Exhibitions/Expos) events.

Choosing the Right Resort for Corporate Outings

Organizing a successful corporate outing starts with choosing the right resort that caters to your specific needs. Consider the following factors when selecting a resort for your corporate outing:

Location and Accessibility: 

Opt for a resort that is easily accessible for all team members. Ideally, it should be located within a reasonable distance from the city center or the company's office.

Facilities and Amenities: 

Ensure that the resort offers a wide range of facilities and amenities that align with your event requirements. These may include spacious event spaces, audiovisual equipment, Wi-Fi connectivity, recreational areas, and on-site dining options.

Event Spaces and Capacity: 

Evaluate the resort's event spaces and their capacity to accommodate your team comfortably. It is essential to have ample room for activities, presentations, and breakout sessions.

Accommodation Options: 

Look for resorts that provide a variety of accommodation choices to suit different preferences and budgets. The availability of different room types such as standard rooms, suites, and villas ensures that every participant can find a comfortable lodging option.

Budget Considerations: 

Determine your budget for the event and find a resort that offers value for money without compromising on quality and amenities.

Benefits of Corporate Outings in Resorts:

Corporate outings in resorts offer a multitude of benefits that contribute to team building, employee engagement, and overall productivity. Some key advantages include:

Team Building and Bonding Opportunities: 

Resort settings provide an environment conducive to team bonding and relationship-building. Engaging in activities together helps foster trust, collaboration, and camaraderie among team members.

Break from the Office Environment: 

A change of scenery is essential for employees to recharge and gain fresh perspectives. Resorts offer a peaceful and serene ambiance away from the usual office setting, allowing participants to relax and rejuvenate.

Enhancing Communication and Collaboration: 

Corporate outings create an informal atmosphere where employees can communicate openly and freely. Interacting in a relaxed setting helps break down barriers, encourages effective communication, and fosters idea sharing.

Boosting Employee Morale and Motivation: 

Corporate outings are a great way to recognize and appreciate employees' hard work. The fun and engaging activities, along with the appreciation from the organization, boost employee morale, motivation, and job satisfaction.

Creating Lasting Memories and Experiences: 

Corporate outings provide opportunities for team members to create shared memories and experiences. These shared experiences strengthen bonds, increase loyalty towards the company, and create a positive work culture.

Area83: The Ideal Choice for Corporate Outings, Events & Celebrations

When it comes to corporate outings, celebrations and MICE events, Area83 is a standout resort in Bangalore. Its unique features, exceptional accommodations, and state-of-the-art event spaces make it an ideal choice for hosting such occasions. Let's explore what sets Area83 apart:

Fun Games and Team Building Activities at Area83

A resort with a wide range of fun games and team building activities adds excitement and thrill to a corporate outing. Bangalore offers several resorts that cater to various preferences and interests. Let's explore some of the thrilling games and activities available in these resorts:

  • Archery Tag: A thrilling combination of archery and dodgeball, where teams compete to eliminate opponents by hitting them with foam-tipped arrows.

  • Bubble Soccer: Participants wear inflatable bubbles and play soccer, adding a hilarious twist to the game.

  • Dirtcart: An exhilarating off-road go-karting experience that takes participants through challenging terrains and tracks.

  • Giant Swing: A high-adrenaline activity where participants are strapped into a harness and swing from great heights.

  • Human Foosball: A life-sized version of the classic table game, where participants are attached to poles and play soccer.

  • Kayaking: A water activity where participants paddle across serene lakes or rivers, enjoying the beauty of nature.

  • Keypunch: A team-building activity that requires participants to communicate and coordinate to solve a series of challenges and puzzles.

  • Low Rope Course: A series of physical and mental challenges designed to build teamwork, trust, and problem-solving skills.

  • Magic Turtle: A unique and interactive game that tests participants' creativity, collaboration, and decision-making abilities.

  • Meltdown: A fun and competitive game where participants try to avoid being knocked off their feet by a rotating arm.

  • Paintball: An adrenaline-pumping team-based activity where participants engage in simulated combat using paintball guns (minimum age requirement: 18).

  • Pipeline: A strategic game that requires teams to construct a pipeline using limited resources, emphasizing teamwork and communication.

  • Pyramid Builder: A collaborative activity that involves building a pyramid structure using limited materials, focusing on coordination and problem-solving.

  • Rocket Ejector: A thrilling ride where participants are propelled into the air using a powerful ejector mechanism.

  • Sky Swing: An exhilarating swing experience that allows participants to soar through the sky, enjoying breathtaking views.

  • Superman: A high-speed zipline activity where participants fly through the air, experiencing an adrenaline rush.

  • Tree Climbing: An adventure activity where participants climb trees using ropes, harnesses, and professional guidance.

  • Water Cycle: An enjoyable water-based activity where participants navigate through the water using pedal-powered cycles.

These games and activities cater to different interests and skill levels, ensuring that there is something for everyone to enjoy.

Area83 Features: 

Area83 is spread across 11 acres and offers an impressive 18-acre private lake, surrounded by lush greenery, creating a serene and picturesque atmosphere. The resort provides ample space for activities, ensuring that attendees can move around comfortably and interact with ease. The fresh natural air and tranquil surroundings contribute to a refreshing and rejuvenating experience. Additionally, Area83 offers ample parking space, nursing rooms for working parents, and a delightful lake-side dining experience where attendees can enjoy delicious meals in a scenic setting.

Event Spaces: 

Area83 boasts spacious and well-equipped event spaces designed to cater to a variety of corporate and Mice Events. The venues are equipped with state-of-the-art technology, audiovisual systems, lighting, and sound equipment to ensure seamless event execution. The ample space provided by the event spaces allows for customization and branding opportunities, creating a personalized environment for each event.

Luxurious Accommodations: 

Area83 offers a range of luxurious accommodations, including chalets, suite rooms, and villa houses. Each accommodation option is meticulously designed to provide maximum comfort and relaxation. The spacious rooms and modern amenities allow attendees to unwind and recharge after a day of activities and celebrations.

Networking and Socializing: 

In addition to its event spaces and accommodations, Area83 provides inviting common areas such as the lobby, lounge, and restaurants. These spaces are designed to create a spacious and welcoming ambiance, perfect for attendees to network, socialize, and build connections with colleagues and industry peers.

Conclusion: 

When it comes to organizing a successful corporate outing or annual day celebration, choosing the right resort is paramount. Bangalore offers an array of resorts that provide breathtaking landscapes, luxurious accommodations, and thrilling activities. Among them, Area83 stands out as an exceptional choice with its unique features, spacious event spaces, luxurious accommodations, and serene surroundings.

Area83 offers a spacious and productive environment for corporate and MICE events, providing a luxurious and comfortable setting that promotes productivity and relaxation. The resort's event spaces are equipped with modern technology and amenities to ensure smooth event execution, while the accommodations and common areas provide ample space for attendees to unwind, network, and socialize.

Make the most of your corporate outing or annual day celebration by choosing the best resort in Bangalore. Unleash the fun, engage your team in exciting activities, and create lasting memories in a resort that combines the perfect blend of relaxation, adventure, and productivity.
