Premier Resort with Outdoor Activities in Bangalore

Events | Jul 12 2023 | By Rafsal Ahammed
  • share:

In the enchanting city of Bangalore, blessed with a harmonious blend of rich culture, history, and cosmopolitan charm, one destination stands out for the thrill-seekers, nature lovers, and those who simply desire a rejuvenating retreat. Welcome to Area 83, a marvel among resorts in Bangalore and your new hub for a unique concoction of leisure, nature, and adrenaline.

Why Choose Area 83: A Different Class of Resorts in Bangalore

Located amidst the serene landscapes just on the outskirts of the city, Area 83 is not your ordinary resort. Setting itself apart from other resorts in Bangalore for family & couples, we offer a captivating mix of tranquility, excitement, and luxury under the same roof. We champion the spirit of adventure, fostering a perfect setting for day outings in Bangalore, weekend retreats, or even extended stays.

Dive into the World of Outdoor Activities

A pulsating heart of adventure, Area 83 stands out as an exceptional Adventure resort in Bangalore. We've curated a catalogue of thrilling outdoor activities, designed to cater to varying levels of adventure cravings. Whether you're a thrill-seeker or prefer a more relaxed connection with nature, our activity offerings are second to none.

Archery Tag & Paintball

Relish the thrill of the age-old sport of archery with a modern twist in Archery Tag. Armed with foam-tipped arrows, teams go head-to-head in this safe yet exhilarating sport. Or immerse yourself in Paintball, the ultimate test of teamwork and strategy. Please note, participants for Paintball must be 18 years and above.

Bubble Soccer, Human Foosball, & More

Unleash your inner child with Bubble Soccer, where the classic game of football gets a hilarious and bouncy twist. Participate in Human Foosball, a larger-than-life version of the popular table game, making you an integral part of the exciting action.

Off-road Adventures: Dirt Kart & Water Cycle

Experience the thrill of off-roading with our Dirt Kart activity. Traverse the rugged landscapes of our resort in a robust dirt kart, satisfying your need for speed in a safe, controlled environment. Alternatively, opt for our unique Water Cycle, combining the joy of cycling and boating into a delightful water-based adventure.

Skyward Thrills: Sky Swing, Giant Swing, & Rocket Ejector

Our Sky Swing, Giant Swing, and Rocket Ejector are sure to satiate your thirst for skyward thrills. Feel the adrenaline rush as you're launched into the air, enjoying panoramic views of our expansive resort and its natural surroundings.

Team Building Activities: Keypunch, Pipeline, & Pyramid Builder

For corporate teams, families, or friend groups, our team-building activities like Keypunch, Pipeline, and Pyramid Builder foster camaraderie, collaboration, and communication.

Enjoy the Calmness of Nature: Kayaking, Boating, & Tree Climbing

For those who prefer tranquility, we've got you covered with activities like Kayaking and Boating, allowing you to unwind on our pristine water bodies. Or choose to connect with nature through Tree Climbing, challenging your physical strength whilst immersed in the resort's verdant greenery.

Magic Turtle & Meltdown: Fun for All Ages

Everyone, regardless of age, will find joy at our Magic Turtle and Meltdown activities, providing endless laughter and bonding moments for families or groups of friends.

A Resort for Day Outing in Bangalore Like No Other

Area 83 offers an exciting, refreshing alternative for resorts for day outing in Bangalore. With a myriad of activities and an inviting, serene ambience, a day spent here is more than just an outing—it's an adventure packed with memories you'll cherish.

Resorts in Bangalore for Family & Couples: An Unforgettable Experience

At Area 83, we ensure every guest, whether families or couples, enjoys an unforgettable experience. As one of the leading resorts in Bangalore for family & couples, we strive to make every moment special, be it through our exhilarating activities or our luxurious amenities.

Nature Resort with Outdoor Activities  Near Bangalore

Nestled within the verdant outskirts of the city, Area 83 also makes for an ideal nature resort near Bangalore. Far from the madding crowd, enveloped in the soothing hues of nature, this is your sanctuary to relax, recharge, and rejuvenate.

In Conclusion: Your Adventure Begins Here

So, why wait? Step into a world of adventure, camaraderie, and relaxation at Area 83, the epitome of Adventure resort in Bangalore. Whether you're seeking a family outing, a romantic retreat, a corporate team-building event, or simply a day of adrenaline-filled fun, Area 83 welcomes you to the adventure of a lifetime. Be ready to be inspired, be thrilled, be rejuvenated. Your adventure begins here.
