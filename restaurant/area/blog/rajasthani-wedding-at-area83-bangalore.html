Experiencing a Rajasthani Wedding at Area83

Events | Sep 28 2023 | By Rafsal Ahammed
  • share:

At Area83, we believe in turning dreams into reality, especially when it comes to celebrating love. A Rajasthani wedding is like no other, with its regal customs and grandeur that leave a lasting impression. Allow us to take you on a journey through the opulence of a Rajasthani wedding, where every detail is thoughtfully curated to make your special day a royal affair.

The Magnificent Baraat Procession

A grand procession that's a feast for the eyes.

In a Rajasthani wedding, the Baraat procession is a spectacle to behold. The groom, riding a beautifully adorned horse or elephant, leads the procession accompanied by family and friends. At Area83, we provide a majestic setting for this grand entrance, complete with traditional music, dhol players, and colorful decorations. Your guests will be mesmerized by the sight and sounds of the Baraat as it approaches the wedding venue, setting the tone for a truly regal celebration.

The Sacred Pheras: Binding of Two Souls

A timeless ritual that signifies eternal union.

The heart of any Rajasthani wedding is the Pheras, where the couple takes seven rounds around the sacred fire, each round signifying a vow and commitment. At Area83, we create a serene and elegant setting for this beautiful ritual. Our outdoor venues are perfect for the Pheras, with the natural surroundings enhancing the sanctity of the moment. The vibrant colors of Rajasthani attire against the backdrop of nature create a breathtaking visual spectacle.

The Joyful Celebration: Music, Dance, and Folk Performances

An explosion of color, music, and dance.

Rajasthani weddings are known for their vibrant and joyful celebrations. At Area83, we ensure that your wedding is filled with the spirit of Rajasthan. Traditional folk musicians and dancers will entertain your guests, creating an atmosphere of merriment and celebration. From the energetic Ghoomar dance to soulful melodies, our Rajasthani performers will add that authentic touch to your special day.

A Feast Fit for Royalty

Savor the flavors of Rajasthan.

No Rajasthani wedding is complete without a sumptuous feast. At Area83, we offer a delectable Rajasthani cuisine that will leave your guests raving. From dal baati churma to gatte ki sabzi, our culinary experts curate a menu that reflects the rich culinary heritage of Rajasthan. Let your guests savor the flavors of royalty as they indulge in a gastronomic journey.

The Perfect Venue for Your Rajasthani Wedding

Choosing the right venue for your Rajasthani wedding is crucial, and Area83 understands the importance of this decision. Our resort, nestled in the heart of nature, provides a royal setting for your celebrations. Whether you prefer a traditional outdoor ceremony or an indoor affair, our versatile spaces can accommodate your every need. As a wedding resort in Bangalore, we ensure that your Rajasthani wedding is an experience to remember.

Experienced Team: Your Wedding, Our Expertise

Planning a Rajasthani wedding can be intricate, but at Area83, we have a team of experienced wedding planners who are dedicated to making your dream wedding a reality. From organizing customs to coordinating logistics, we take care of everything, allowing you to focus on what truly matters – your love and commitment to each other.

Capture the Royal Moments: Memories to Cherish Forever

A Rajasthani wedding is a once-in-a-lifetime event, and we believe that every moment should be captured. Area83 provides access to professional photographers and videographers who specialize in wedding photography. Let them capture the grandeur and the grace of your Rajasthani wedding, ensuring that your memories are preserved for generations to come.

Your Dream Rajasthani Wedding Awaits

Area83 is not just a wedding venue; it's a place where dreams come true. With the grandeur of Rajasthan, the warmth of tradition, and the beauty of nature, we offer a setting that is perfect for celebrating love. Let us be a part of your royal journey as you embark on this new chapter of life together. Your dream Rajasthani wedding awaits at Area83, where love is celebrated with regal customs and opulence.
