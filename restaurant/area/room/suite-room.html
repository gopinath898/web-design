Description

Comfort is important when you are away from home, so choosing accommodations that offer the best amenities throughout the duration of your stay should be a no-brainer. A way to achieve this is by reserving one of our suites. Our suites are spacious and stocked with extra comforts to make your stay pleasant.

Experience comfort amidst nature by staying in our aesthetically crafted, cozy suite rooms. The exquisitely designed luxury rooms nicely complement the modern interiors' mellow warmth and let in plenty of natural light. Welcome your mornings at the resort by taking a stroll along our lake. The ripples provide a subtle background score that perfectly embodies rest and tranquility.

The opulent suites at Area 83 effectively combine traditional natural elements, such as a private pool, with state-of-the-art contemporary amenities, like a private dining room, a bathtub, and other features. The interior is a richly coloured, thermally adaptable space created to give you a cozy, classic appearance that is nevertheless rustic.

Note: (For Chalets and Suites) 

If the number of in-house guests exceeds 15 people, there will be no option to order à la carte; a buffet will be provided. If the number of in-house guests is less than 15, we allow the guests to order à la carte.

FAQ's

  1. Can I make a reservation for a specific room at Area 83?

    Yes, you can make a reservation for a specific room at Area 83, subject to availability. Be sure to mention your preferences when making your reservation.

  2. What amenities are included in the rooms at Area 83?

    All rooms at Area 83 come with a private bathroom, air conditioning, flat-screen TV, and comfortable bedding. Some rooms may also include additional amenities such as a electrical kettle room service in suite, iron box with iron stand, and in-room safe.

  3. Is room service available for rooms at Area 83?

    Yes, room service is available for rooms at Area 83. Guests can enjoy a variety of delicious meals and snacks from the comfort of their own room.

  4. Is there a minimum stay requirement for rooms at Area 83?

    Yes, there is a minimum stay requirement for rooms at Area 83. The minimum stay varies depending on the season and the type of room booked, so be sure to check with the resort when making your reservation.

  5. Are there any age restrictions for guests staying in rooms at Area 83?

    There are no specific age restrictions for guests staying in rooms at Area 83. However, children under the age of 18 must be accompanied by an adult.

  6. Is parking available for guests staying in rooms at Area 83?

    Yes, there is ample parking available for guests staying in rooms at Area 83. The resort offers free on-site parking for all guests.
