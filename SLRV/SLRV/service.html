Scope of Work Undertaken

1. Authorized Service Center handling Port Installation and Manufactures Warranty Service.

2. Technical Training Center for Low Voltage Components.

3. Service of Low Voltage Components.

4. Annual Maintenance Contracts for Low Voltage Switchgear.

5. Supply & Installation of Spare.

6. Emergency Breakdown Assistance.

7. Retrofit of Obsolete & Old Switchgear Components.

8. Procurement Agency for third Party Spares & Systems.

SCOPE OF WORK

• Cleaning of Breaker with CRC-226.

• Removal of old Grease and Re-Greasing the same recommended grease.

• Checking the condition of arcing contact and gap between fixing and moving arcing.

• Contacts shall be checked and adjusted as per the recommendations.

• Checking the Proper Function of Mechanism.

• Checking Tripping of ACB through protection Release.

• Checking presence and proper function of Mechanism.

• Checking presence and proper tightening of hardware.

• Checking condition and alignment of jaw contact with credible terminals.

• Checking the healthiness of jaw contact and cradle terminals.

• Checking alignment of SIC.

• Checking scrapping earth alignment.

• Checking condition of arc chutes.

• Checking ACB tripping through shunt release.

• Checking motor operation in case of EDO ACB.

• Checking closing coil operation in case of EDO ACB.

• Checking continuity and proper changeover of aux contact lock.

• Checking of release by release testing kit PR 010 for LSIG Function.

• Setting of release as per the load.

• Checking the healthiness of CTs and its connectors TS.

• Recommendation of spares, if required.

In case of any faulty part taken out of any switchgear and replaced, the same shall be handed over to you. Any other defect observed during service, which will affect the performance of switchgear shall be recorded by us and we shall give a copy of the same to you for records and further action.
