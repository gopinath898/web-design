About us

Established in 1974 by our founder Mr. Sawarmal Agarwal, Calcutta Tube Centre is a registered distributor for G.I, M.S Pipes, G.I, C.I, MS, GM Valves Fittings, and PVC, CPVC Pipes & Fittings. With headquarters in Bengaluru, India. The company has made a mark over the last four decades in the plumbing and construction industry. At Calcutta Tube Centre, we recognize the importance of sustainable practices and our responsibility towards the environment. Calcutta Tube Centre has created a niche by understanding and catering to the needs of leading builders, construction companies, engineers, and corporates. With a wide network of dealers across Karnataka, we specialize in a complete range of high-quality plumbing materials, engineering works, agricultural valve fittings, pipes, and rainwater harvesting systems. We take pride in being the preferred choice of thousands of customers for our variety and service.
